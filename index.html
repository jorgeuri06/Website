MH Hacks

Morgan Hill's Very Own Hack Club!

About

Interested in coding, building cool projects, and making new friends? MH Hacks is Morgan Hill's Hack Club we are a hybrid club, meaning that we hold meetings in various places around Morgan Hill. Everyone - from total beginners to experienced coders - is welcome. Join our inclusive community to learn programming, collaborate on websites, apps, games, and more. No experience required, just bring your curiosity and passion for creating! It's the perfect place to discover the world of tech while having fun!
